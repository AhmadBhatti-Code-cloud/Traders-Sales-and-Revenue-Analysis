# Traders-Sales-and-Revenue-Analysis
This repository contains a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard built on sample e-commerce data. It provides insights into revenue by category, average order value by customer, monthly sales trends, and discount distribution. The dashboard helps identify top-performing segments, seasonal patterns, and discount impacts on sales.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Research Questions :


Q1 : Which segments contribute the highest share of total revenue?


Q2 :  Who are the top customers based on average order value, and what does this indicate about purchasing behavior?


Q3 :  Which product categories generate the highest revenue, and how significant is the gap between top and low-performing categories?


Q4 :  How does revenue fluctuate over time, and are there noticeable seasonal or monthly trends?


Q5 :  What is the relationship between discounts and overall revenue performance? Do higher discounts lead to higher sales volume?


Q6 : Are there concentration risks in the business, such as heavy dependence on a few customers or specific segments?
